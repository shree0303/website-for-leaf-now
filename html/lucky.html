The money plant is also known as the jade plant.

It’s a generally undemanding houseplant, which makes it perfect for busy plant parents and first-time owners.
The Jade plant requires direct sunlight or full sun to grow for at least four hours
every morning. It needs to be watered once a week. Keep the soil moist, but don’t overwater it.
